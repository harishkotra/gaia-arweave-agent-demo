About This App

Chat with a Gaia AI model and store your conversation permanently on the Arweave permaweb.

How to Use:
  1. Type your message and click Send or press Enter.
  2. Use Undo to remove your last message and the AI's response.
  3. Click Upload Chat to Arweave to save the conversation.
